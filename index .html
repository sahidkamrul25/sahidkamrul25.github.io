About

Sahid Kamrul is a writer, columnist, and former university lecturer. Currently a researcher at Freie Universität Berlin, he specializes in postcolonial English literature, North American studies, cultural theory, ecosystem discourse, partition history, South Asian politics, theology, and comparative religion.

His work explores the intersections of identity, power, belief, and memory—spanning both contemporary and traditional issues. His official academic name is Md Sahidul Islam, under which he publishes scholarly articles and academic work.

Research Interests

  • Ecosystems & Environmental Humanities
  • Partition History & Memory
  • Politics & Comparative Theology

Creative & Cultural Work

  • Awarded poet and performer in national poetry recitation competitions
  • Fluent in 7 languages, including Bangla, English, Arabic, Urdu, Hindi, and more
  • Professional presenter and moderator in academic, literary, and cultural events

Publications & Media

Selected short stories and literature reviews by Sahid Kamrul have been published in prestigious national newspapers such as The Daily Star (Bangladesh). His writing engages with themes of memory, identity, and power through both creative and critical lenses.
